About LMC

What is the LUX MUNDIS CUP 2024?

The Lux Mundis Cup is an annual event at the school where the school community showcases their skills, talents, and creativity. From the preparations to the actual performances, participants deliver their best. Through this event, students once again demonstrate their originality, talents, and capabilities. Since it is a competition, everyone puts in extra effort to achieve the rewards of their hard work. Overall, it's an opportunity for everyone to try their best and give their all.

What was your experience during the event?

The opening salvo of the Lux Mundis Cup was a blast!, especially with the speaker being extra loud—I liked it a lot. After performing our Pagarbuhay presentation, the comments from our institute were so pleasant that I felt all those late nights were worthwhile. It was an great experience, especially with the bond we formed as Pagarbuhay participants.


On the second day, we had our softball game. It was funny and quick, but I had a great time. The remaining days were truly memorable, with people showcasing their talents and hard work through spectacular performances. Overall, it was an amazing event—except for the last day, which I didn't quite understand.

Tip

"Seize every opportunity, especially as a first-year, to get involved and create lasting memories. The journey is just beginning!""
